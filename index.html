GearStorage: Your Ultimate Backpacking Companion

GearStorage is your go-to app for organizing and optimizing your outdoor adventures or usual trips. Seamlessly track and manage all your travel essentials, from boots and jackets to climbing tools and accessories. Customize each item with details like model, brand, size, quantity, photo, description, and weight. Create your own Gear Lists to be ready to go on a trip at any moment.

Key Features

Be always ready for a new adventures.

  • Organization

    Keep all your stuff in one place with detailed information and photo.

  • Custom Categories

    Customize your gear organization with personalized categories for easy navigation.

  • Gear Lists

    Prepare for any adventure by crafting specialized gear lists. Whether it's a three-day winter hiking expedition or a weekend camping trip, ensure you have everything you need.

  • Total Weight Calculation

    Stay on top of your pack weight.

Import file format:

• comma separated .csv file

• no headers required, just data

• 5 fields: ModelName, Notes, Brand, Size, Weight (in grams)
